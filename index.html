About Me

Benjamin Wulf is a System Administrator turned fiction writer. Author of Shell Gaming Dominion, he mainly writes psychological techno-thrillers with a sub genre of cyberpunk. His first novel is being adapted into a graphic novel.

He currently is attending DU's Web Programming bootcamp. Looking to re-enter the world of tech, he looks forward to working hard and getting results.

He is an avid golfer, youth golf instructor, and when not studying always down to tackle a tee time.
